## About MediaBubble

MediaBubble is a results-driven digital marketing agency located in **Hurghada, Egypt**. Since 2015, we have been helping businesses in **Real Estate, Hospitality, Tourism, Diving, and Healthcare** stand out in competitive markets through integrated, high-ROI marketing strategies.

From the shores of the Red Sea, we combine deep local expertise with global standards to deliver measurable growth for our clients.

### What We Do

- **Performance Marketing** — SEO, Google Ads (PPC), Social Media Advertising
- **Branding & Strategy** — Brand identity, positioning, and storytelling
- **Creative & Content** — Photography, videography, social content, and campaigns
- **Web Development** — High-converting, modern websites and digital experiences
- **Social Media Management** — Strategy, content, community, and paid amplification

We specialize in the **Red Sea tourism and real estate** sectors, helping hotels, resorts, dive centers, property developers, and service businesses thrive in Egypt and expand internationally.

## This Repository

This is the official **digital platform workspace** for MediaBubble:

- **Next.js 14 Marketing Website** (bilingual: English + Egyptian Arabic Masri)
- **Interactive Brand Guidelines App** (`/brand`)
- **Complete documentation & strategy** under `docs/`
- **OpenCode AI Agent ecosystem** (Arabic-first)

### Key Features

- ✅ Bilingual (EN + Masri Arabic) with full RTL support
- ✅ Modern Next.js 14 App Router + Tailwind CSS
- ✅ Interactive Brand Guidelines at `/brand`
- ✅ Service pages, Portfolio, Blog, Contact with HubSpot integration
- ✅ Conversion-focused UX (Newsletter, Floating CTA, etc.)
- ✅ SEO optimized with sitemap, metadata, and JSON-LD

## Tech Stack

- **Framework**: Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + custom brand tokens
- **i18n**: i18next (English + Egyptian Arabic)
- **Deployment**: Vercel
- **Forms & CRM**: Resend + HubSpot
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4
